About The Watches

Audemars Piguet

Class

The Audemars Piguet Royal Oak was launched in 1972 and caused controversy at the Basel Watch Fair. Created by Gerald Genta, who supposedly drew the initial design overnight, the Royal Oak caused a stir in the industry with its unconventional design and its high price for a steel watch. The now iconic octagonal bezel, the exposed screws and the integrated bracelet undoubtedly made the Royal Oak attract attention, but like everything that is ahead of its time

JBW Saxon

Timeless

Introducing the Saxon 10 Year Anniversary Edition. Originally designed for the first collection of JBW, this 2018 edition holds true to the Saxon legacy. The updated faceted bezel and case features 16 genuine diamonds, housing a Japanese movement with multiple functions, such as day, dual-time, and 24hr indicator. The Saxon uses unique materials like mother-of-pearl dial accents and a croc-embossed genuine leather strap. A compelling timepiece redefined by a decade of history.
